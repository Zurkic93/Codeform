About Me

Hello! I’m Zlatko Zurkic, a passionate and detail-oriented Full-Stack Web Developer with over 4 years of hands-on experience building dynamic, user-friendly websites and web applications.

My expertise spans both front-end and back-end development, allowing me to craft seamless, responsive experiences for users while ensuring that the underlying code is efficient, maintainable, and scalable for future growth.

I specialize in creating custom solutions that go beyond templates. Whether it's a fully dynamic web app using React.js on the front-end, Node.js on the back-end, or a powerful content management system like WordPress, I take pride in bringing ideas to life with clean, well-structured code. My development approach is rooted in industry best practices, ensuring every project I touch is fast, secure, and ready for future challenges. Over the years, I’ve had the opportunity to collaborate with a diverse range of clients—from entrepreneurs launching their first product to established businesses looking to improve their online presence. My clients value my ability to understand their vision, break it down into actionable steps, and deliver a product that doesn’t just work, but truly shines. I love helping people turn their ideas into beautiful, functional digital experiences that engage users and grow businesses.

When we work together, you can expect clear communication, attention to detail, and a relentless focus on delivering exactly what you need—whether it's a responsive website that looks great on any device, a web application that handles thousands of users seamlessly, or ongoing support to ensure your project continues to perform at its best. If you’re looking for someone who is as invested in your project’s success as you are, I’m your guy. I’m here to solve problems, build custom solutions, and ensure that your website or application is ready to take on the future.

Let’s create something amazing together!
